**Recommendation: SELL**

Here's why: The JLT studio market is approaching saturation. Supply of studios in JLT and nearby areas (JVC, Barsha Heights) has increased significantly. While your yield is solid, capital appreciation is likely to plateau or soften. You've made a 31-38% gain since 2019 — now is a good time to lock in profits.

Estimated net proceeds after fees: **AED 510,000 - 535,000**

## Unit 2: 1-Bed — Dubai Marina, Cayan Tower

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Purchase Price (2021) | AED 980,000 |
| Current Market Value | AED 1,350,000 - 1,400,000 |
| Capital Appreciation | +38-43% |
| Annual Rent | AED 85,000 |
| Gross Yield | 6.2% (on current value) |
| Service Charge | ~AED 16,000/yr |
| Net Yield | ~5.0% |

**Recommendation: HOLD**

Marina is evergreen — consistently strong rental demand, low vacancy, and one of the most liquid markets in Dubai. The yield has compressed due to price appreciation (which is a good problem to have), but Marina properties rarely lose value long-term. Cayan Tower is iconic and always in demand. Hold this one.

---

## Unit 3: 2-Bed — Business Bay, Executive Tower K

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Purchase Price (2022) | AED 1,350,000 |
| Current Market Value | AED 1,600,000 - 1,650,000 |
| Capital Appreciation | +19-22% |
| Annual Rent | AED 105,000 |
| Gross Yield | 6.5% (on current value) |
| Service Charge | ~AED 18,000/yr |
| Net Yield | ~5.3% |

**Recommendation: HOLD (with caveat)**

Business Bay 2-beds are solid performers. The area has matured significantly and demand from young professionals and couples is strong. I'd hold for now.

The caveat: Executive Tower is an older building (completed 2012). As newer towers come online in Business Bay, your unit may face competition. In 2-3 years, consider selling and upgrading to a newer building in the same area.

---

## Reinvestment Strategy

If you sell the JLT studio (proceeds ~AED 520K) and want to redeploy:

**Option A:** Add AED 520K to savings and buy a 1-bed in Dubai Hills (Park Point) for ~AED 1.5M. This gives you exposure to one of Dubai's fastest-appreciating communities.

**Option B:** Go off-plan in Creek Harbour. AED 520K covers the down payment on a 2-bed with a 60/40 plan. By handover in 2026/27, the unit could be worth 15-20% more than purchase price.

**Option C:** Keep it liquid and wait for a deeper market correction. If prices soften another 5-10% in the next 6 months (possible but not certain), you'll have cash ready to deploy.
